# 🤖 AI Quality Documentation Assistant

> "Helping quality professionals transform observations into objective, evidence-based documentation while preserving human judgment."

---

# Overview

Lumina is an AI-powered Quality Documentation Assistant designed to support Quality Assurance professionals throughout the documentation process.

Rather than replacing evaluators or making quality decisions on their behalf, Lumina acts as an intelligent documentation partner that encourages structured reasoning, objective writing, and consistent documentation practices.

Every evaluation remains guided, reviewed, and approved by a human evaluator.

---

# Our Philosophy

Quality documentation is not simply about recording mistakes.

It is about creating meaningful documentation that supports:

- Employee growth
- Fair evaluations
- Coaching conversations
- Operational consistency
- Organizational learning

Artificial intelligence should strengthen these goals—not replace the professionals responsible for them.

---

# Human-in-the-Loop AI

Lumina follows a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) design philosophy.

The evaluator remains responsible for:

- Making quality decisions
- Selecting evaluation outcomes
- Verifying observations
- Reviewing AI-generated documentation
- Approving final documentation

AI assists.

Humans decide.

---

# How Lumina Works

Instead of immediately generating documentation, Lumina guides evaluators through a structured reasoning process before drafting documentation.

✨ Generate

# 🛡️ AI Safeguards

Lumina is designed with responsible AI principles.

AI should never:

- Invent observations
- Create evidence that does not exist
- Override evaluator decisions
- Score interactions automatically
- Replace human judgment

Instead, Lumina exists to improve documentation quality while maintaining evaluator ownership throughout the process.
---
# ❤️ Why Lumina Is Different

Many AI writing tools begin with generation.

Lumina begins with reasoning.

Rather than asking:

"How can AI write this?"

Lumina asks:

"How can AI help the evaluator think more clearly before writing?"

This human-centered approach promotes fairness, transparency, consistency, and professional judgment while reducing repetitive documentation tasks.
